# ⏳ Vulnerabilities Timeline

- **1988** – Morris Worm (first major internet worm, exploited buffer overflow)
- **1996** – Aleph One’s “Smashing the Stack for Fun and Profit”
- **2000** – Format string vulnerabilities discovered in real-world software
- **2014** – Heartbleed exposes millions of servers
- **2017** – WannaCry & NotPetya highlight devastating RCE exploits
- **2020+** – Modern browsers and kernels harden against UAF & BOF
